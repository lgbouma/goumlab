Blog purpose:

Welcome! This blog is a place to share and discuss some of the wonderful things that I'll be learning in graduate school. I'm a physics student. Most of what I expect to write about here will cover:

  • Astrophysics
  • Exoplanets
  • Doing cool things with computers (physics, statistics, art, music, ...)

If what I have to say about these topics (or others, as we go along) interests you, awesome! Posts in learning will exhibit what I'm processing in my coursework and reading, while posts in computation and exoplanets will be tailored more broadly towards code that I write, or research ideas that I'm hashing out.
